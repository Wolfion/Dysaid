Introduction

Thank you for using Dysaid Extension. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Extension. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.


Information We Collect

The Extension operates entirely within your browser and does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data processing happens locally on your device.


Local Data Processing

  • Content Access: The Extension reads all the content on the web page in order to modify its style.
  • User Data: Any data that the Extension processes remains on your device. The Extension does not transmit any information to external servers or third parties.
  • Usage Data: Information such as settings, preferences, and interactions with the Extension are stored locally on your browser and are not shared with us or any external entities.

No Data Sharing

We do not collect or share any data with servers, third parties, or any other external entities. All operations performed by the Extension are confined to your browser environment.


Data Security

Since the Extension does not transmit data outside your browser, there is minimal risk of data breaches or unauthorized access. However, it is important to use the Extension in a secure and updated browser environment to ensure the safety of your local data.


Your Consent

By using the Extension, you consent to our privacy policy.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
